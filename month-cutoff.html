🗓️ Inventory Month Cutoff

What this does

Snapshots current On Hand / location quantities into each item's Opening Balance for the next period, then archives all Movement Log, Purchase Order, Sales Order, Invoice, and Consumption history so far. Nothing is deleted — history stays queryable, just flagged as archived.

⚠️ This closes the current inventory period. It is safe to re-run (idempotent), but should only be run when you actually intend to close the period.
